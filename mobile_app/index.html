About this dictionary

This is a personal frequency dictionary from "48 Laws of Power". Words are sorted by frequency, and study mode focuses mostly on advanced vocabulary.

How to use it effectively:

  • Press "Today" and learn one word at a time.
  • Try to recall the meaning first, then open translation.
  • Open IPA for difficult words.
  • Use Next to continue to the next word.
  • Use Remove to exclude a word from study.
  • Use Review for due words.
  • Use Stats to track progress and achievements.
